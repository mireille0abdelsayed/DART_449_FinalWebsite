Dear younger me,

As I sit down to write this letter, my heart is filled with a mix of emotions, nostalgia, reflection, and a deep sense of longing to connect with you, my younger self. I want to take this opportunity to share with you the wisdom and insights I've gained over the years, to offer guidance and comfort, and perhaps most importantly, to remind you that you are never alone on this journey. So, let's embark on this journey together, as I share with you the lessons and experiences that have shaped who I am today.

xoxo,
Your older self

Do you remember the endless summer days spent exploring the world around us, the simple joy of laughter, and the warmth of family during childhood? Those were the days when every moment seemed to hold a new adventure, and the world was filled with endless possibilities. As I look back on those cherished memories, I am filled with gratitude for the carefree days of our youth and the love that surrounded us.

Do you remember your adolescence?


It was a period of growth, discovery, and navigating the tumultuous waters of change. It was during these formative years that we faced some of our greatest challenges and learned invaluable lessons about resilience, self-discovery, and the power of perseverance. Through the highs and lows, the triumphs and setbacks, we emerged stronger and wiser, armed with the knowledge that we could overcome any obstacle that came our way. My dear younger self, I want you to know that even in the darkest of times, you are stronger than you know. When life throws its toughest challenges your way, remember to draw strength from within, to lean on the love and support of those who care about you, and to never lose faith in yourself. You have already faced adversity with courage and resilience, and you will continue to do so, emerging stronger and more resilient with each passing storm.

But look at yourself in the mirror and look at how far you’ve come.

I am so proud of the woman you’ve become.

As I come to the end of this letter, I want to leave you with a final message of encouragement and hope. My dear younger self, you've got this. You have already faced so much with courage, resilience, and grace, and I have no doubt that you will continue to do so with each new challenge that comes your way. Trust in yourself, believe in your abilities, and never forget that you are capable of achieving greatness. So, embrace the journey, cherish the moments, and always remember that I am here with you every step of the way.

The End

About

This project is a personal letter written from me to my younger self. It is everything I would have wanted to tell my younger self to give her hope. That we will get over all the challenges we have been through and that she will be just ok. At the end of the webiste, that mirror, is a fast-forward to my younger self. For her to see how far she's come and see everything she has accomplished. If only she knew that as a kid...
